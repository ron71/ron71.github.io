Data Governance Certified Champion

Issued by Informatica

The Informatica Data Governance Certified Champion status allows an individual to demonstrate competence in the Data Governance landscape.

By attending the prudently crafted Enablement Session and working through scenarios based hands-on labs, and successfully executing a Proof of Concepts (POC) workshop designed on Informatica Data Governance Suite, the individual acquires the skill required to become Data Governance Certified Champion.

The Informatica Data Governance Certified Champion credential confirms business acumen and technical knowledge needed to design, engineer, implement, and manage the overall Data Governance practice of an organization.

This week long workshop based session provides both Business and Technical value with comprehensive hands-on experience around Axon with a special focus on how it integrates with the other key components of Informatica Data Governance Platform.
